Privacy Policy – Jazz Progression Trainer

Effective date: April 5, 2025

Overview

Jazz Progression Trainer does not collect, store, or share any personal data. No account is required to use the app.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal information, usage data, or analytics.

Internet Usage

The app connects to the internet solely to load piano audio samples from a public CDN (tonejs.github.io). No user data is transmitted in this process.

Third-Party Services

The app uses Tone.js and Salamander Grand Piano samples, both of which are open-source resources. No third-party analytics or advertising SDKs are included.
